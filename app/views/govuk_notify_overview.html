Quicker, cheaper and easier

Government receives millions of calls every year from people anxious to find out about the progress of their application or whether we received their payment. People have to spend time on hold, which wastes their time and costs government a lot of money in call centre running costs.

GOV.UK Notify makes it easier to keep people updated, by helping service teams across government to send text messages, emails or letters to their users.

Benefits to service teams include:

  • send emails, text messages and letters easily
  • easy templating for each message you want to send
  • cheaper than most commercial providers - free email sending and an allowance of free text messages for each service
  • flexibility and resilience by having a number of SMS, email and post providers
  • specific permission levels for different team members

Benefits to day-to-day operations include:

  • API integration with your web applications or back office systems
  • simple web interface to upload batches of messages
